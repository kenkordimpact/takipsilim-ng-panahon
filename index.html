Takipsilim ng Panahon

/

Kenkord Impact

From messages on stone walls, to birds in flight carrying letters, to parchment, quills, and ink, to newspaper printing and to articles on the internet, the world has always been evolving, and the way we pass around information has evolved as well. Kenkord Impact was able to utilize the technology within their grasp to create a digital zine that includes different aspects of writing to deliver information and brand-new concepts to the global age.

Our digital zine is entitled Takipsilim ng Panahon which talks about the things that have happened to us in the pandemic. The zine itself consists of three parts: Neverland Stasis, which talks about how we are overshadowed in the dawn of the pandemic. Overmorrow is about how we, as people learn to fight for hope and freedom in the world. And lastly, We Travel Universes: The Universe of ▮▮, is the part where we talk about people and how we think of them.

In addition to the digital zine, we have released a trailer, a mini film, and a playlist that is closely related to our creative concept.
